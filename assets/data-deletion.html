Dad Bod Account and Data Deletion Request

Last updated: March 25, 2026

You can request deletion of your Dad Bod account profile and associated app data by contacting us.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email from your registered Gmail ID used in Dad Bod.
  2. Use subject line: Delete my Dad Bod account data.
  3. Include your name and Gmail ID used in the app.
Email Deletion Request

What Gets Deleted

  • Profile details associated with your app account on your device.
  • Meal logs, workout logs, weight entries, and progress photos stored by the app.
  • Optional AI settings tied to your app profile on the device.

Retention and Processing Time

We typically process deletion requests within 7 business days.

Some data may remain temporarily in local device backups controlled by your device or platform provider until those backups rotate.

Self-Service Local Deletion

You can also remove locally stored data immediately by clearing app storage in your device settings or uninstalling the app.
