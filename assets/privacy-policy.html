Dad Bod Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

Overview

Dad Bod is designed to keep your data on your device. Core tracking data, including meals, workouts, weight logs, and progress records, is stored locally in app storage.

Data We Process

  • Account profile details you enter (name, email)
  • Health and fitness data you log (nutrition, workouts, weight, photos)
  • Optional AI inputs when you choose AI features

How Data Is Used

  • To provide fitness and nutrition tracking features
  • To generate progress insights and workout planning
  • To support optional AI meal and photo analysis only when you trigger it

Third-Party Services

If you enable optional AI features, relevant input text may be sent to the AI provider using your own API key. Exercise media lookups can use Tenor for GIF guides.

Data Sharing

Dad Bod does not sell personal data. Data is not shared with advertisers.

Data Deletion

You can clear app data from within the app or by uninstalling the app.
