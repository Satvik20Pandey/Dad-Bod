Dad Bod Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

1. Who We Are

Dad Bod is a fitness and nutrition tracking app published by Satvik Pandey. This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled when you use the app.

2. Information We Process

  • Profile details you enter, such as name and email.
  • Fitness and health entries you create, such as meals, workouts, weight, and photos.
  • Optional voice input and text prompts if you choose those features.
  • Optional API key details you provide for AI features (stored locally on your device).

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide nutrition, workout, and progress-tracking functionality.
  • To generate summaries, insights, and charts in the app.
  • To process optional AI features only when you trigger those actions.

4. Where Data Is Stored

Core app data is stored locally on your device (for example, browser/app local storage). Dad Bod does not run a mandatory central account server for core tracking features.

5. Third-Party Services

Dad Bod may interact with third-party services only for optional features you use:

  • AI processing providers (for example, OpenRouter-connected models) when you submit AI prompts or analysis requests.
  • Tenor (or similar media providers) for exercise GIF/media lookup.

Those providers may process data according to their own privacy policies. Review their policies before using those features.

6. Data Sharing and Selling

Dad Bod does not sell your personal data. We do not share personal information with data brokers or advertisers for targeted advertising.

7. Children’s Privacy

Dad Bod is not intended for children under 13 without parent or guardian supervision and consent. If you believe a child provided personal data without permission, contact us and we will help remove it from the device workflow where possible.

8. Your Choices and Data Deletion

  • You can edit or delete entries directly in the app.
  • You can clear app data in your device settings.
  • You can uninstall the app to remove locally stored app data.

For formal deletion requests, use: Account and Data Deletion Request

9. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect information handled by the app. No method of storage or transmission is fully guaranteed to be secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

11. Contact

If you have privacy questions or requests, contact: satvikofficial20@gmail.com
