## Full description
Dad Bod is a premium fitness and nutrition companion built for real-world consistency.

Track your daily calories, protein, carbs, fats, and key micronutrients. Log meals with AI-powered estimation from plain English descriptions, voice input, or manual entry when you already know the numbers.

Features:
- Luxury dark UI designed for clarity and focus
- AI meal estimation via your own OpenRouter key (GPT-4o Mini recommended)
- Voice meal logging with live transcript preview
- Structured weekly gym split with timers and form cues
- Morning cardio + abs circuit tracking
- Weight trend chart and progress photos
- Weekly meal planner
- Export/import your data anytime

Privacy first: your logs stay on your device. AI features only run when you add your own API key.

Built Dream Physique.
