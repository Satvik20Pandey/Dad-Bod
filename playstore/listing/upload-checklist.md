# Play Store Upload Checklist

1. Upload AAB
- playstore/artifacts/DadBod-v1.0.2-signed.aab

2. Store Listing Text
- Short description: playstore/listing/short-description.txt
- Full description: playstore/listing/full-description.txt
- Release notes: playstore/listing/release-notes.txt

3. Graphic Assets
- Icon 512x512: playstore/assets/icon/app-icon-512.png
- Feature graphic 1024x500: playstore/assets/feature-graphic/feature-graphic-1024x500.png
- Phone screenshots: playstore/assets/screenshots/phone/
- Tablet screenshots: playstore/assets/screenshots/tablet/

4. URLs
- Privacy policy URL
- Support URL
(see playstore/listing/play-console-config.md)

5. App Content
- Category, ads declaration, target audience
- Data Safety form (see playstore/listing/data-safety-form.md)
- Content rating questionnaire

6. Final Verification
- Internal testing rollout first
- Check pre-launch report
- Promote to production staged rollout
