Dad Bod is a clean, practical fitness companion built for daily consistency.

Track your meals, calories, macros, and full nutrient profile in seconds. Follow a structured morning and evening workout plan, log weights used in gym sessions, and monitor your progress with weight charts and photos.

What you can do in Dad Bod:
- Log meals quickly with calories, macros, and micronutrients
- Track full nutrition: protein, carbs, fat, fiber, sugar, sodium, potassium, vitamins, and more
- Use voice input for meal logging
- Use optional AI-assisted meal estimation
- Follow split-based evening gym workouts
- Track morning cardio and abs session completion
- Use integrated timers and exercise GIF guides
- Record body weight and view trend charts
- Save progress photos over time
- Export strength workout history as CSV
- Export and import your app data

Built for real use:
- Mobile-first interface
- Fast tab navigation
- Compact session-based workout layout
- Works without subscription

Privacy-focused by design:
- Core data is stored locally on your device
- Optional AI features run only when you trigger them

Dad Bod helps you stay consistent, measurable, and focused on results.
